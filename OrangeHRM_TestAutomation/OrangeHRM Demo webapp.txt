For automating test cases on the OrangeHRM demo application, consider the following:

Login Functionality: Verify valid and invalid login with correct/incorrect credentials.
Navigation Menu: Test if all menu items are clickable and lead to the correct pages.
User Profile: Test adding, editing, and deleting user profiles.
Employee Management: Add, edit, and delete employee details.
Time and Attendance: Verify attendance and time-related features.
Search Functionality: Test search for employees, users, and other data.
Reports: Generate and validate reports like employee data, leave reports.
Logout: Ensure proper session termination.
